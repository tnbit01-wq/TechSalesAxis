## How TalentFlow Works (3-Step Journey)

### **For Candidates** 🚀
```
1. Sign up & choose experience level (Fresher → Leadership)
2. Upload resume → Parse skills
3. Take AI Assessment (6-16 questions, 30-45 min)
   ↓
4. Get Trust Score (0-100)
   ↓
5. Unlock dashboard → Browse verified jobs
   ↓
6. Apply → Get invited → Schedule Jitsi interview → Get hired
```

### **For Recruiters** 💼
```
1. Sign up & verify company (CIN/GSTIN)
2. Gemini AI auto-generates company bio
3. Answer 5 strategic questions about hiring ethics
   ↓
4. Get Company Trust Score (CPS)
   ↓
5. Unlock dashboard → Post jobs or discover talent
   ↓
6. See shortlisted candidates → Message → Schedule interview → Hire
```

## Key Features

### **Assessment Engine**
- **Adaptive difficulty:** 6 Qs (freshers) → 16 Qs (leadership)
- **AI Scoring:** Google Gemini evaluates answers 0-6 per rubric
- **Anti-Cheat:** Tab-switch detection, copy-paste blocking, 60-second timers
- **Transparent Results:** Component scores (behavioral, skill, psychometric)

### **Resume Intelligence**
- **Groq Llama Parsing:** Real-time extraction of roles, skills, gaps, education
- **AI Verification:** Actual experience validated against assessment answers
- **PDF Generation:** Multiple templates, interactive builder for freshers

### **Hiring Pipelines**
- **Pipeline A (Applied):** Traditional job-based applications
- **Pipeline B (Recommended):** AI suggests candidates matching job requirements
- **Global Pool:** Search all 100% verified candidates by skill, experience, location

### **Virtual Interviews**
- **Jitsi Integration:** One-click free video meetings
- **UTC Scheduling:** No timezone confusion; all times standardized
- **Slot Management:** Recruiter proposes → Candidate confirms → Auto link generation

### **Real-Time Communication**
- **AWS RDS Chat:** Real-time messaging via WebSockets (FastAPI) with database polling
- **Elite Constraint:** Recruiters init conversations; prevents cold messaging
- **Thread History:** All interaction audited for compliance

### **Notifications (Signal Center)**
- **Channels:** Email, in-app, mobile push (user-configurable)
- **Types:** Application updates, messages, security alerts, interview confirmations
- **Deep Linking:** One-click navigation to relevant dashboards

### **Career GPS (Market Intelligence)**
- **Skill Demand:** Which skills are hot in your market
- **Your Ranking:** Performance vs. other verified candidates
- **Salary Trends:** Benchmark pay for your experience level
- **Career Paths:** Next-step roles and upskilling recommendations
